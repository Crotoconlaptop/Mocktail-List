Golden Sands

Description:
Immerse yourself in the perfect harmony of dark cane, strawberry, and basil cordial, complemented by the citrusy embrace of lemon mango and crowned with a luscious mango foam. Ideal for an aperitif, it kickstarts your evening with a symphony of refreshing flavors that dance on your palate.

Ingredients:

  • Dark Cane (Lyres) 50ML
  • Strawberry & Basil Cordial 25ML
  • Lemon 25ML
  • Mango & Mango Foam Top

Glass: Low Ball

Garnish: Coffee Powder Line

Method: Shake

Preparation:

Shake all ingredients and double strain and top with the mango foam

And All That Jazz

Description:
Embark on a sensory journey with this intricate mix of sage and basil cordial, saline solution, orange blossom water, and a touch of sparkling magic. Perfect as an aperitif, it awakens your palate with a blend of herbal notes and citrus, setting the stage for an evening filled with vibrant experiences.

Garnish: Yellow Jelly Ball

Method: Build

Preparation:

Build and stir direct into the glass.


Comments:

Saline Solution: 80ML of water-20g salt

IV

Description:
Indulge in a delightful concoction that weaves together the sweet notes of amarettis, the tanginess of orange juice, and the warmth of vanilla-yuzu, all crowned with a velvety foam topping. Best enjoyed as a dessert cocktail, it provides a sweet and satisfying conclusion to your meal, leaving a lingering taste of indulgence.

Ingredients:

  • Amaretti (Lyres) 60ML
  • Orange Juice 40ML
  • Vainilla-Yuzu 35ML
  • Foamee 5ML

Glass: Retro Martini

Garnish: 4 Bitters

Method: Dry Shake & Stain

Preparation:

Shake all the ingredients without ice (dry shake) then strain direct to the glass.

Just In Thyme

Description:
Refresh your senses with this versatile blend of peach and thyme cordial, enhanced by the zing of lemon juice, pineapple juice, limoncello, and American malt. Whether as an aperitif or a digestive, its herbal and citrus-infused profile ensures a harmonious experience, making it a delightful choice for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • Peach Thyme Cordial 60ML
  • Lemon Juice 25ML
  • Pineapple Juice 80ML
  • Limoncello (use Lime-Sherbit Syrup) 30ML
  • American Malt (Lyres) 10ML
  • Fresh Thyme 3 pcs.

Glass: Rock Glass

Garnish: Fresh Thyme & Grilled Pineapple

Method: Muddle, Shake & Stain

Preparation:

Muddle the fresh thyme to get the flavor then put all the ingredients except the pineapple juice, shake, serve the pineapple juice in the glass and slowly add the content of the shaker.

Oud Elixir

Description:
Indulge in the luxurious Oud Elixir, featuring a unique blend of fresh peach, cardamom, and cloves reduction, with a touch of balsamic raspberry shrub. Ideal for digestion, it offers a warming combination of spices that takes you on a journey of sophisticated and exotic flavors.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh Peach Blend 60ML
  • Cardamom & Dates Puree 60ML
  • Lime Juice 25ML
  • Balsamic Raspberry Shrub 20ML

Glass: Gold Mug

Garnish: Banana Leaf, Raspberry

Method: Shake & Strain

Preparation:

Put all the ingredients to the shaker then shake and double strain.


Comments:

Balsamic Raspberry Shrub:

  • 150gr sugar
  • 170gr raspberrys

Muddle the raspberries with sugar, let them rest overnight, and add balsamic vinegar.

The Regal Mary

Description:
Unleash bold flavors with the Regal Mary, a royal twist on the classic Bloody Mary. Tomatoes, Tabasco, Worcestershire sauce, dates puree, and a hint of cloves and cardamom reduction, finished with Himalayan salt and black pepper. A bold choice for an aperitif, setting the tone for a flavorful meal.

Ingredients:

  • Tomato Juice 160ML
  • Riyadh Bloody Mix 30ML
  • Dates reduction 10ML
  • Cloves & Cardamom reduction 10ML
  • Himalayan Salt Pinch
  • Ground Black Pepper Top

Glass: High Ball

Garnish: Kefir Lime, Cherry tomato & Rim of Sumac powder

Method: Shake & Strain

Preparation:

Put all the ingredients to the shaker then shake and strain.


Comments:

Riyadh Bloody Mix:

  • 180ML Worchester sauce
  • 30ML Tabasco
  • 20gr Horseradish paste
  • 20gr Majorane
  • 5gr Fenugreek powder
  • 2gr Cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of Tumeric powder

Blend all ingredients together and then fine strain.


Coriander & Clove Reduction:

  • 125ML sugar
  • 250ML water
  • 15gr coriander
  • 6gr cloves

Sous vide all ingredients for 1.5 hours al 60 degrees.
Strain, then let the reduction cool before use.


Date Reduction:

  • 10 pcs of seedless dates
  • 200ML of water

Sous vide all ingredients for 1 hour at 60 degrees.
Once cooked, blend the contents, and let the reduction cool.
